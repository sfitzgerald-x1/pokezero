# Gen3 randbats interaction inventory — engine-enumerated

Status: audit, 2026-07-04. Generated by interrogating the vendored
Showdown gen3 Dex against the generator's `sets.json`: the reachable
universe is **113 moves, 76 abilities across 220 species, 13 items**
(item universe documented in
[`observation_compression_design.md`](observation_compression_design.md)).
Every mechanic class below is mapped to its spec disposition:
**covered** (rule exists), **inert** (unreachable in this format), or
**DECIDED HERE** (gap found by this audit, rule stated). Anything not
listed carries no special mechanics (plain damage/status/boost moves).

## Abilities — by spec channel (all 76 reachable, grouped)

**Damage modifiers (CB max-explanation set / residual conditioning):**
Guts(6), Hustle(3), Huge Power(1), Pure Power(1) — candidate-max
confounds, as specced. Overgrow/Blaze/Torrent/Swarm (17 total) —
pinch boosts at ≤⅓ HP: attacker HP is public → **deterministic
conditioning, not confounds**. Thick Fat(7), Marvel Scale(1) —
defender-side, public-conditioned (status public) → conditioning plus
defender-ability inference channel. Plus/Minus — **inert in singles**.

**Absorb/immune class (negation + reveal + non-trigger elimination):**
Volt Absorb(2), Water Absorb(6), Flash Fire(5), Levitate(11),
Wonder Guard(1), Damp(4), Soundproof(3 — near-inert: the pool's only
sound moves are Perish Song/Heal Bell class). Status-immunity family:
Immunity, Limber, Water Veil, Insomnia, Vital Spirit, Own Tempo,
Oblivious, Magma Armor — block events are announced → ability reveal;
successful infliction prunes the blocker from candidates (non-trigger
family).

**Contact procs — positive-reveal ONLY:** Static(6), Poison Point(4),
Effect Spore(2), Flame Body(2), Cute Charm(3), Rough Skin(1). All
`[from] ability`-tagged on proc. **DECIDED: explicitly excluded from
non-proc pruning — procs are probabilistic (~30%), so absence is not
evidence** (the tautology principle's boundary, stated to prevent a
future "optimization").

**Trapping:** Shadow Tag(1), Arena Trap(1), Magnet Pull(2) — trapper-
alive flag family, as specced.

**Weather:** Drizzle/Drought/Sand Stream — covered (source rules).
**Cloud Nine(1)/Air Lock(1) — DECIDED:** while revealed-and-active they
suppress all weather *effects* (residuals, Synthesis amounts, sand
chip): one conditioning bit on the public-modifier set; reveal is
announced on switch-in.

**Speed modifiers:** Swift Swim(11), Chlorophyll(9) — 20 species;
validates the turn-order-inference removal. Speed Boost(2) — public
boost events, no rule needed.

**End-of-turn/identity:**
- Shed Skin(2), Rain Dish(1): `[from]`-tagged, attribution hygiene only.
- Natural Cure(6) — **DECIDED, deterministic elimination:** a statused
  mon switches out and later appears status-free with no public
  Heal Bell/Aromatherapy in between ⇒ Natural Cure confirmed (all
  cure paths are public events in this pool). Joins the exact-state
  identification family.
- Early Bird(7) — **DECIDED, one deterministic case:** Rest sleeps
  exactly 2 turns; a Rest-wake after 1 turn ⇒ Early Bird confirmed
  (extends the Rest-flag rule). Natural-sleep wake timing stays soft
  evidence (1–4 turn range overlaps).
- Trace(2), Truant(1), Color Change(1), Forecast(1), Synchronize(6),
  Intimidate(11): all announced/tagged — existing machinery.
- Pressure(16): PP ledger ×2 rule, as specced.

**Inert in battle:** Run Away, Pickup, Illuminate, Stench, Keen Eye*,
Sturdy (no OHKO moves), Plus/Minus (singles).
(*Keen Eye matters only against accuracy drops; no accuracy-dropping
moves are in the pool — verified.)

**No spec impact (probabilistic modifiers):** Sand Veil, Compound
Eyes, Serene Grace, Shell Armor/Battle Armor (crit-blockers change
crit *rates*, and the residual conditions on the crit *flag*, not the
rate), Shield Dust, Inner Focus, Hyper Cutter/Clear Body/White Smoke
(stat-drop blocks are announced → reveal channel only).

## Secondary effects (complete census: 27 entries over 113 moves)

All secondaries are protocol-announced when they fire. Dispositions:

- **Mud Shot is the pool's only 100% target secondary** (guaranteed
  spe −1) — and therefore the format's single deterministic
  Shield-Dust detector: normal damage lands, no drop event fires,
  announced blockers (Clear Body/White Smoke are `[from]`-tagged) and
  `hit-sub` outcomes excluded ⇒ Shield Dust confirmed. Joins the
  non-proc family as its one move-secondary member; valid *only*
  because the chance is 100%.
- **Every other target secondary is ≤50%** (10% brn/par/frz on the
  elemental moves, 20–30% drops/flinches/confusion, Sacred Fire 50%)
  ⇒ positive-reveal-only; non-firing is never evidence (probabilistic
  boundary, verified move-side).
- **Self-effects** (Meteor Mash/Silver Wind/Steel Wing raises;
  Overheat/Psychoboost/Superpower drops; Hyper Beam recharge) are
  public boost/volatile events — residuals already condition on
  current public stages; no rules needed.
- **Interactions:** flinch surfaces as `|cant|flinch`; Synchronize
  reflections are tagged; Serene Grace doubles chances
  (probabilistic → soft); gen-3 Substitute blocks target secondaries,
  so secondary non-fire on `hit-sub` is masked from all inference
  (including the Mud Shot rule).

## Format simplifications this audit certifies

1. No OHKO moves, no semi-invulnerable turns, no rampage locks, no
   2–5 multi-hits, no accuracy-dropping moves, no Taunt/Torment/
   Disable/Encore-adjacent move-locking except Encore itself, no
   weight- or team-dependent powers (Low Kick/Beat Up absent).
2. Exactly one of each: drain move, multi-hit move (fixed 2), charge
   move, recharge move, slot condition, side condition.
3. The full conditional-power set is {facade, solarbeam, pursuit,
   flail, reversal, hiddenpower, return} — seven moves, each with a
   stated disposition.

## Per-token historical context (DECIDED — principled derivability exception)

Transition tokens carry a **context trio: {own spikes layers, opponent
spikes layers, weather id}** at that turn. Rationale: these are global
fields whose *historical* values are recoverable only by replaying
events through time — exactly the state-tracking burden transition
tokens exist to remove — and they materially reinterpret actions
(Rapid Spin's value ↔ layers at that moment, switch chip ↔ layers,
Thunder/Solar Beam ↔ weather). This is the mechanism for the net to
*discover* the spin↔spikes correlation from history rather than
memorize it. Capped at these three by construction; anything else
re-grows the snapshot.

## New rules this audit added (delta to the design doc)

Return whitelist-inclusion at 102; Facade/Solar Beam public
conditioning; charge-turn token rule; Trick item-state mutation;
move-trapping as public volatile; contact-proc non-proc exclusion;
Cloud Nine/Air Lock suppression bit; Natural Cure elimination;
Rest+Early Bird identification; Damp/boom non-trigger symmetry;
Sturdy-is-dead (pruning must not await its evidence).
